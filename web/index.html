The green lines are the car's distance sensors - that's all it can see. It was never told where the track is; it learned which sensor patterns mean "turn now" by crashing thousands of times and getting a small reward for progress. Across 30 fresh tracks-seeds it had never trained on, the trained policy completes a clean lap 100% of the time with zero crashes; the untrained brain and a hand-coded heuristic crash within the first corner (see runs/ppo/eval.json).
